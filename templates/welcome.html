How it shows the way ?

Lighthouse is an idea that can ease the life of visually impaired people in the modern scenario.
The primary objective of our project Lighthouse is to design an entirely new mechanism that allows system to differentiate between humans and machines but in such a way that it also provides a feasible solution for the visually impaired community.
It also deals with all security aspects of CAPTCHA validation and capable of providing a mechanism which is user friendly for the people with visual imparities or people who have low vision power.


Guiding the ships...

The objective of the “completely automated public turing test” popularly known as CAPTCHA is to differentiate humans from automated agents by requesting the solution to a problem that is easy for humans but difficult for computers.
CAPTCHAs are used to prevent automated agents from performing any malicious act by restricting their access to web resources.


Brick, Mortar and Lights !

The working principle behind the project is usingthe touch and feel concept for CAPTCHA validation instead of visual.
The entire mechanism starts with the activation of our service on the device by clicking a simple button.The mobile of the user should have the service installed in this which will ask the user to shake the mobile or tap at a specific position in order to validate it.
